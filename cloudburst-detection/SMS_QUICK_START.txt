========================================
TO ACTUALLY SEND SMS (Optional)
========================================

Takes 10 minutes to setup:

1. Sign up: https://www.twilio.com/try-twilio
   (Free account, $15 credit)

2. Get Twilio phone number
   (Free with trial)

3. Copy Account SID & Auth Token
   (From Twilio dashboard)

4. Create .env.local file:
   Copy-Item env.example.txt .env.local

5. Edit .env.local with your credentials:
   NEXT_PUBLIC_TWILIO_ENABLED=true
   NEXT_PUBLIC_TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID=ACxxxxx...
   TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN=your_token_here
   NEXT_PUBLIC_TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER=+1234567890

6. Restart server:
   npm run dev

7. Check admin panel:
   Should show "‚úÖ SMS service is configured"

8. Test with YOUR phone number!
